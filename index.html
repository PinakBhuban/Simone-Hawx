Our Offices

At Goldman Sachs, we understand that you can’t be global without being local. Our on-the-ground presence spans more than 60 cities around the world.

Dallas

Our Dallas office is our third largest in the US and is located in the Dallas Arts District downtown, the largest arts district in the nation. Recently renovated, the office features flexible workspaces to foster collaboration and a wellness exchange which offers a range of services to support our peoples’ health and well-being.

Learn More

Birmingham

The firm has opened a new office in the heart of the UK to attract talent from a diverse and highly skilled population. See why we are there and explore job openings.

Learn More

Hyderabad

Opened in 2021, our Hyderabad office is our newest location in India and, true to the region’s dynamic and innovative atmosphere, is in growth mode. The office is home to a variety of teams across our businesses, and includes key global functions led on the ground here.

Learn More

New York

In New York, great things can be found in unexpected places — just take a peek outside our global headquarters at 200 West Street. We’re in Battery Park City, which is filled with quintessentially New York City offerings. From a local rooftop bar to great restaurants and retail shopping, many of the city’s highlight attractions are right outside our doorstep.

Learn More

Warsaw

Goldman Sachs’ Warsaw office is located in the Warsaw Spire building, opened in 2016, which is the tallest office building in Poland. The workspace is designed to support collaboration and flexibility by featuring unassigned seating.

Learn More

Tokyo

Established in 1974, our Tokyo office is the firm’s oldest in Asia. Located in the vibrant Roppongi Hills neighborhood, it is home to a childcare facility, wellness and meditation rooms, and a variety of clubs including flower arrangement, sign language, skiing and snowboarding.

Learn More
